About EcoSwap

Making the world more sustainable, one swap at a time.

Who We Are

EcoSwap is a community-driven platform that encourages people to **swap, share, and donate** everyday items rather than discarding them. Our goal is to **reduce waste, promote sustainability, and build a circular economy** where resources are shared responsibly.

How It Works

Swap & Save

Connect with users and arrange the exchange or donation.
